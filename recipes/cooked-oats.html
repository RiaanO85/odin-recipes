Cooked Oats

Description

Sometimes basic is better. At breakfast, that can certainly be the case.

These easy oatmeal recipes teach you the basic methods so you get creamy, tender oats every time. The flavorings and toppings are up to you.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil the water in a kettle
  2. Place the cup of oats and salt into a microwaveable bowl
  3. Mix in the boiled water
  4. Microwave for 2 minutes on high, stirring every 30 seconds
  5. Serve warm
