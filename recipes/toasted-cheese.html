Toasted Cheese

Description

Everyone needs to know how to make a classic grilled cheese sandwich. Whether you're a beginner cook or an old pro, you'll come back to this top-rated grilled cheese recipe again and again.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 20g butter

Steps:

  1. Place a pan on medium heat
  2. Spread one side of each bread slice with an even coat of butter
  3. Lightly coat the pan in oil
  4. Place one slice of buttered bread into pan
  5. Sprinkle both cheeses onto that slice
  6. Place other slice on top, buttered side facing up
  7. Flip after 3 minutes, or when golden brown
  8. Serve warm
